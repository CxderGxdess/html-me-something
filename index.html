The Norwegian Sky

My first cruise experience took place aboard the beautiful Norwegian Sky). This ship had everything you can imagine and more. I was able to lounge all day at the pool. This ship had two pools and five hot tubs. Also, there was a free open bar and lots of onboard fun like the dance floor and all the shows in the Stardust Lounge. There was a theater where I saw a couple of comedy shows and laughed the night away.

Delicious Dining

All resturaunts onboard were free. I ate a bit too much because of this. My favorite resturaunts on the ship were:

  • The crossing
  • The Palace
  • Garden Cafe
  • Le Bistro

I was able to be seated with my favorite waiter Johnson. He was awesome and so much fun. There were counless food options for breakfast, lunch and dinner. I tried a little of everything. I think I miss the food the most :)

Islands We Visited

Stirrup Cay

The first Island we visited was the Great Stirrup Cay. I enjoyed this stop more than I anticipated. It was a nice private beach with beautiful clear blue water. I relaxed on the beach all day and drank margaritas form the unlimited open bar. I was an amazing day that I will never forget.



The Bahamas

The Bahamas was absolutly amazing! The beaches were beautiful, the food was great, and the natives were very friendly. I made lots of new friends. It was very overwhelming, there was so much to see and do. One day was not enough to take in this remarkable place. I will definately visit again.

Bahamas Stay

Although I did not spend the night in the Bahamas, there are many wonderful hotels and resorts to choose from should you decide to go for yourself.

This was a great getaway that gave me incredible pictures and momories of a lifetime
